Poppy Seed Noodles

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of egg/potato noodles
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (or however much you want really)
  • 1 1/4 cup poppyseeds
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • however much ground black pepper you want too

Description

Mohnnudeln (meaning poppy seed noodles in German), is the name of thick noodles of a potato dough in Bohemian and Austrian cuisine, similar to the Schupfnudel. The main difference is, that Mohnnudeln are served with melted butter, ground poppy seeds, and sprinkled with confectioner's sugar.

Directions

  1. Place noodles in pot with water
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Grind poppy seeds and sugar in small coffe grinder, be sure to scrape sides
  4. Place drained noodles in warmed bowl
  5. Add poppy seeds, sugar, salt, and pepper to melted butter
  6. Pour over noodles
  7. Taste the Hungarian tradition!!!
